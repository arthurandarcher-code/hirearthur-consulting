Professional photography for every menu item, corrected pricing that was leaving money on the table, and negotiated fees down — in one case from 15% to 6%.

03
Digital Presence & Paid Acquisition

Improved Google Maps rankings for multiple competitive keywords. Implemented Google Ads driving direct sales conversions and expanding the business's web presence. Continuously optimized the digital stack for better features and performance at lower cost.

Distributor Strategy — $150k Saved

Nurtured a relationship with a major food distributor rep who audited the client's existing invoices — uncovering roughly $150k in annual savings on the exact same items. Established a second distributor for both better pricing and supply chain contingency.

Menu & POS Optimization

Operations & Cost Cutting
